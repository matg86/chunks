How it works

Productivity isn't about motivation. It's about planning, organisation and discipline.

Chunks is a place where you can plan out your day's tasks into... you guessed it... Chunks!

You won't find anything complicated here. Just a clean, efficient planner that leaves you to get on with the task at hand

The Process

Step 1:

Note down 1 main task. For example, "Complete Company XYZ website". Don't get too specific here. That part comes in a minute...

Step 2:

Note down the Chunks that you need to get done to finish your task. For example: Gather necessary images / Add required Fonts / Run code through a linter etc...

Step 3:

Get on with it. By breaking a task down in to smaller chunks you will find your self speeding through your to-do list.
